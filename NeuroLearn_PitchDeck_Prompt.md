## Project Overview

Create a professional, modern pitch deck for **NeuroLearn** - an AI-powered learning companion designed specifically for neurodivergent individuals (ADHD, Autism, Dyslexia). This is for a hackathon/investor presentation.

## Design Requirements

### Visual Style

- **Modern, clean, and accessible** - no overwhelming visuals
- **Color palette**: Deep purples (#667eea), soft blues (#5d9cec), with accents of green (#22c55e) and orange (#f59e0b)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter, Poppins, or similar)
- **Glassmorphism elements** with subtle gradients
- **Plenty of white/dark space** - neurodivergent-friendly design
- **Icons**: Simple, rounded line icons (Lucide/Feather style)

## Additional Design Tips

1. **Use high contrast** - accessibility matters
2. **Limit text** - max 6 bullet points per slide
3. **Big fonts** - minimum 24pt for body, 44pt for headlines
4. **Animations** - subtle, not distracting
5. **Consistent spacing** - clean and organized
6. **Include alt-text** - for accessibility
